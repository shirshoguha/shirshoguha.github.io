Salmon

A Salmon is any of seven different species in the Salmoninae subfamily. The seven species are the Pink, Chum, Koho, Masu, Chinook, Sockeye, and Atlantic. Close relatives include whitefish, trout, grayling, and char. You have a wide range of selection to choose when you arrive at Chilli's.
